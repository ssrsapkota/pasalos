# Breathing Room

**Concept:** Letting content rest.

Densely packed information can be overwhelming. "Breathing room" refers to the feeling of spaciousness that prevents cognitive overload.

## Guidelines
- **Padding is your friend:** When in doubt, add more padding.
- **Line Length:** Restrict text line length (measure) to 65-75 characters. The space left over is breathing room.
- **Images:** Don't crowd images with text; give them a frame of empty space.

## Emotional Effect
- **Calmness:** Open space feels calm.
- **Premium:** High-end brands use lots of breathing room.
- **Trust:** A cluttered interface can feel "spammy" or cheap.
