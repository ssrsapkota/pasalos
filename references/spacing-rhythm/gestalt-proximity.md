# Gestalt Proximity

**Concept:** Closeness implies relationship.

The Law of Proximity states that objects that are near each other tend to be grouped together.

## Application
- **Grouping:** If you want users to perceive a headline, text, and button as a single "card" or "unit", place them closer to each other than to the surrounding elements.
- **Separation:** Be careful not to place unrelated items too close, or the user will mistakenly assume they are related.

## The "Container" Fallacy
You don't always need a border or a background color to define a group. Sufficient white space between groups (and tight space within groups) is often cleaner and more elegant.
