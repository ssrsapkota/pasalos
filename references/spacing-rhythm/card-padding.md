# Card Padding

**Concept:** Internal container spacing.

Card padding protects the content from the edges of the container, ensuring readability and visual comfort.

## Guidelines
- **Consistency:** Use the same padding on all sides for a balanced look (e.g., `p-6`).
- **Hierarchy:**
  - **Small Cards:** `p-4` (16px)
  - **Standard Cards:** `p-6` (24px)
  - **Featured Cards:** `p-8` (32px) or more.

## Mobile Considerations
On mobile, you might reduce the padding slightly to save horizontal space, but don't go too low (keep at least 16px).
