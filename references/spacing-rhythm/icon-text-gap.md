# Icon Text Gap

**Concept:** Space between icon and label.

Icons and text are often paired (in buttons, navigation, alerts).

## Standard
- **Small Text:** 6px or 8px (`gap-1.5` or `gap-2`).
- **Large Text:** 12px (`gap-3`).

## Alignment
- Ensure the icon is vertically centered with the text cap-height or line-height.
- Too much gap makes them feel disconnected.
- Too little gap feels crowded and hard to distinguish the shape of the icon.
