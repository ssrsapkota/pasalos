# Negative Space

**Concept:** Using emptiness as a design element.

Negative space (or white space) is not just empty background; it is an active design element that defines structure, hierarchy, and focus.

## Principles
- **Focus:** The more space around an object, the more attention it draws.
- **Luxury:** Generous negative space often connotes luxury, sophistication, and varying quality.
- **Clarity:** Sufficient space prevents visual clutter, making the interface easier to scan and understand.

## Application
- Don't feel the need to fill every pixel.
- Use negative space to separate groups instead of lines or borders.
- Balance the negative space compositionally (e.g., if there is a lot of space on the left, balance it on the right or with a heavy element).
