# Geometric Scale

**Concept:** Doubling progression (2, 4, 8, 16, 32, 64, 128...)

A geometric spacing scale provides a very strong visual rhythm and clearly defined hierarchy. By doubling the values, the difference between steps is always distinct.

## The Progression

- **2px**: Ultra-fine detail (rarely used, borders etc)
- **4px**: Base unit
- **8px**: Standard tight
- **16px**: Standard medium
- **32px**: Standard large
- **64px**: Section break
- **128px**: Major section break
- **256px**: Heavy distinct break (Landing pages)

## When to Use
- When you need high contrast in spacing.
- For layouts that require clear, undeniable separation between elements.
- Often good for brutalist or very structured technical designs.
