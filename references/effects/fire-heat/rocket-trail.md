# Rocket Trail

**Keywords:** Exhaust, Propulsion, Smoke, Fire, Launch.

**Description:**
A bright, fast-moving flame followed by a dense smoke trail.

## Usage

### 1. Particle System
Emitter attached to a moving object.
- **Tip**: Bright white/yellow, short life.
- **Mid**: Orange/Red, medium life.
- **End**: Grey smoke, long life, expanding size.

### 2. CSS Gradient Trail
A conic or linear gradient that fades out behind the object.
