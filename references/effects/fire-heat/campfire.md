# Campfire

**Keywords:** Cozy, Flame, Logs, Camping, Warmth.

**Description:**
A contained, looped fire animation with a central focus. Key elements are the base glow and the flickering tongues of flame.

## Usage

### 1. Sprite Sheet Animation
The most realistic and performant way for a specific fire look is looping a pre-rendered flame sprite sheet.

### 2. SVG Path Morphing
Animate the `d` attribute of an SVG path to simulate the shape of a flame changing.
