# Blood Splatter

**Keywords:** Hit, Damage, Red, Fluid, Decal.

**Description:**
A liquid burst that stains the background or "camera lens".

## Usage

### 1. Canvas / Image Decals
Pre-rendered splatter sprites.
Rotate randomly and place at impact point.
For "lens" splatter: minimal movement, slow fade out, blur.

### 2. Procedural Splatter
Draw a central blob.
Draw "fingers" extending outwards using noise or random walk.
