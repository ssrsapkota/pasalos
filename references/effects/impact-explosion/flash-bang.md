# Flash Bang

**Keywords:** White, Blind, Stun, Fade, Overexposure.

**Description:**
The screen turns completely white instantly and then slowly fades back to normal.

## Usage

### 1. CSS Overlay
A full-screen `div` with `pointer-events: none`.
Start at `opacity: 1`, animate to `opacity: 0`.
Duration: 0.1s in, 2s out.
