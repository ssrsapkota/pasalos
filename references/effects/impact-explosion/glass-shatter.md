# Glass Shatter

**Keywords:** Break, Window, Crack, Sharp, Fragments.

**Description:**
[See Dimension Shatter](../portal-dimensional/dimension-shatter.md).
Specific to glass: shards should be transparent/blue-tinted and sharp.

## Usage

### 1. Voronoi + Physics
Generate Voronoi cells.
On click, apply explosive force from the click position.
Shards should rotate rapidly as they fly.
Opacity fades as they travel.
