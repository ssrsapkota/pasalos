# Note Particles

**Keywords:** Melody, Song, Listening, Float, Music.

**Description:**
Musical notes (♪, ♫) floating up from a source, often swaying side-to-side.

## Usage

### 1. CSS Text Animation
Spawn `div`s with text content `♪`.
Animate `transform: translateY` and `opacity`.
Add `transform: translateX(sin(time))` for sway.
Colors often match album art.
