# Crescendo Build

**Keywords:** Volume, Intensity, Rise, Drop, Anticipation.

**Description:**
A visual ramping up of intensity (brightness, shake, speed) leading to a "drop" or climax.

## Usage

### 1. JS Variable Ramp
Over a period of time (e.g., 8 bars), increase `shakeStrength`, `brightness`, and `particleCount`.
At peak: Trigger [Flash Bang](../impact-explosion/flash-bang.md) and reset variables.
