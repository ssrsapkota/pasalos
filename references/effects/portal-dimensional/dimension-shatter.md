# Dimension Shatter

**Keywords:** Glass, Break, Fragment, Reality, Shard.

**Description:**
The scene cracks like glass, and the shards float apart to reveal darkness or another image behind.

## Usage

### 1. Voronoi Fragmentation
Generate Voronoi cells on the screen plane.
Turn each cell into a separate mesh.
Apply physics to move/rotate them away from a central impact point.
