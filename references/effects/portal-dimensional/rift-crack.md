# Rift Crack

**Keywords:** Tear, Space, Dimensional, Jagged, Glow.

**Description:**
A jagged vertical or diagonal tear in empty space, often glowing from inside.

## Usage

### 1. SVG Path & Filters
A glowing stroke path with `feTurbulence` displacement to make it jagged and alive.
Inside the crack: a masking separate layer showing "stars" or "void".
