# Reality Distortion

**Keywords:** Heat Haze, Wiggle, Dream, Uncertainty, Wave.

**Description:**
A full-screen wave distortion, making the world look unstable or dream-like.

## Usage

### 1. SVG Displacement Map
Apply `<feDisplacementMap>` to the entire body or container, using a `<feTurbulence>` source that animates `baseFrequency` or `seed`.
