# Hail Fall

**Keywords:** Ice, Bounce, Damage, Heavy, Storm.

**Description:**
Fast falling white spheres that bounce when they hit the ground.

## Usage

### 1. Canvas Physics
Similar to [Rain Drops](rain-drops.md) but:
- Particles are opaque circles (not lines).
- Bounce logic at bottom `vy *= -0.5`.
- Sound visualization is often helpful here.
