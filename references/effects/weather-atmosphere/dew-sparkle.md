# Dew Sparkle

**Keywords:** Wet, Morning, Grass, Shine, Glisten.

**Description:**
Tiny pinpoint reflections on surfaces, simulating wetness.

## Usage

### 1. CSS Sparkles
Small white divs with high blur and `mix-blend-mode: overlay`. Animate opacity slowly.

### 2. Specular Highlights (3D)
Increase the specular power/intensity on the material.
