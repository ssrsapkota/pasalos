# Rain Sheet

**Keywords:** Heavy, Downpour, Curtain, Blur, Transition.

**Description:**
A dense wall of rain that obscures vision, often used as a scene transition or intensity marker.

## Usage

### 1. Particles (High Velocity)
Thousands of long, thin streaks.
Motion blur is essential here. In Canvas, draw lines with `globalAlpha < 0.2`.
Accumulate multiple frames without clearing fully to fake motion blur.
