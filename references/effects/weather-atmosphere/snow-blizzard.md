# Snow Blizzard

**Keywords:** Whiteout, Storm, Wind, Cold, Chaos.

**Description:**
High-density snow moving horizontally or diagonally with high speed. Often obscures the background.

## Usage

### 1. CSS Animation
[See Rain Drops](rain-drops.md), but tilt the rotation and increase speed. Add a white overlay with low opacity to simulate the "fog" of snow.

### 2. Canvas Particles
Add strong horizontal wind force and turbulence.
