# Petal Scatter

**Keywords:** Flower, Cherry Blossom, Sakura, Pink, Romance.

**Description:**
Very similar to [Leaf Fall](leaf-fall.md) but with smaller, pink particles and often faster, more chaotic wind.

## Usage

### 1. Particles.js (Cherry Blossom Preset)
- Speed: Medium
- Direction: Diagonal
- Rotation: Random
- Shape: Custom image or small irregular polygon.
