# Sand Blow

**Keywords:** Desert, Wind, Erosion, Grain, Dry.

**Description:**
A stream of beige/brown particles moving horizontally across the screen, often close to the "ground".

## Usage

### 1. Particles
Similar to [Rain Drops](../water-liquid/rain-drops.md) but horizontal `(vx > vy)` and smaller size.
Add turbulence to make it look wind-swept.
