# Vine Grow

**Keywords:** Plant, Spread, Nature, Green, Climbing.

**Description:**
A vine extending and curling around objects or along a path.

## Usage

### 1. SVG Stroke Dashoffset
The classic "draw line" trick, but with a thick green stroke.

### 2. Canvas Procedural
Draw segments that grow from the end of the previous segment, adding slight curvature (perlin noise) and occasionally branching.
