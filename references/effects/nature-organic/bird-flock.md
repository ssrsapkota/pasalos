# Bird Flock

**Keywords:** Murmuration, Group, Flying, Animals, Sky.

**Description:**
A group of distant birds flying in a coordinated pattern, often morphing shapes.

## Usage

### 1. Boids Algorithm
The standard approach (Craig Reynolds).
- **Separation**: Steer to avoid crowding local flockmates.
- **Alignment**: Steer towards the average heading of local flockmates.
- **Cohesion**: Steer to move towards the average position (center of mass) of local flockmates.
