# Dust Motes

**Keywords:** Floating, Particles, Light, Air, Atmosphere.

**Description:**
Tiny, barely visible particles floating slowly in the air, usually only visible when they cross a light source (see [Light Beams](../light-rays/light-beams.md)).

## Usage

### 1. CSS & Noise
A repeating noise texture scrolling very slowly with low opacity.

### 2. Canvas
Small circles with low alpha `(0.1 - 0.3)` moving with Brownian motion (random small steps).
