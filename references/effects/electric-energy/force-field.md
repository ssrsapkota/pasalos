# Force Field

**Keywords:** Barrier, Wall, Deflect, Glow, Interaction.

**Description:**
See [Energy Shield](energy-shield.md). Generally refers to a larger, possibly invisible wall that reveals itself when touched.

## Usage

### 1. WebGL Depth Buffer
In 3D, calculate the intersection of the shield sphere with the scene geometry to create a glowing "rim" where the shield touches the ground.
