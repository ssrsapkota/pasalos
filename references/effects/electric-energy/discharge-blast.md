# Discharge Blast

**Keywords:** Explosion, Release, Shock, Flash, Particles.

**Description:**
The violent release of built-up energy. Similar to [Power Surge](power-surge.md) but more aggressive and directional.

## Usage

### 1. CSS & Particles
A mix of a 1-frame "white out" (flash) and a burst of high-speed particles moving away from the source.
