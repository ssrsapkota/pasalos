# Tesla Coil

**Keywords:** Continuous, Purple, High Voltage, Science, Arc.

**Description:**
A central point emitting multiple bolts of electricity in random directions continuously.

## Usage

### 1. Canvas
Create multiple [Electric Arcs](electric-arc.md) from a central point `(cx, cy)` to random points `(rx, ry)` within a radius. Update `rx, ry` every few frames.
