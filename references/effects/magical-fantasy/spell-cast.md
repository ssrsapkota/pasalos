# Spell Cast

**Keywords:** Projectile, Fireball, Bolt, Hands, Shoot.

**Description:**
The accumulation of energy in hand, followed by a projectile launch.

## Usage

### 1. Charge Phase
[Charge Buildup](../electric-energy/charge-buildup.md) at hand position.

### 2. Launch Phase
Spawn a projectile (sprite) with velocity towards target.
Spawn a [Muzzle Flash/Discharge](../electric-energy/discharge-blast.md).
