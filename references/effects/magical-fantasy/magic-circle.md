# Magic Circle

**Keywords:** Rune, Spell, Floor, Geometry, Glowing.

**Description:**
[See Summoning Circle](../portal-dimensional/summoning-circle.md).
Often used as a "charging" indicator before a spell fires.

## Usage

### 1. SVG Rotation
Multiple concentric rings rotating at different speeds.
Fade in `opacity` when casting starts.
Expand `scale` from 0 to 1 rapidly.
