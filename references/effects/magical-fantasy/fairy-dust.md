# Fairy Dust

**Keywords:** Tinkerbell, Sparkles, Magic, Childhood, Dream.

**Description:**
Fine sparkling powder that falls from a flying character or magical boject.

## Usage

### 1. Canvas Particles
Similar to [Sparkle Trail](sparkle-trail.md) but affected by gravity.
High drag (floats down slowly).
High shimmer/twinkle rate.
Color: Gold, Blue, Pink.
