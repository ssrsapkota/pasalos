# Curse Effect

**Keywords:** Evil, Skull, Purple, Shadow, Debuff.

**Description:**
Dark, shadowy tendrils or skulls rising from a character to indicate a negative status effect.

## Usage

### 1. Particles (Skull Sprite)
Emit particles with a skull image.
Motion: Slowly rising and drifting.
Color: Dark Purple / Black.
Blend Mode: Multiply (for darkness) or Normal.
