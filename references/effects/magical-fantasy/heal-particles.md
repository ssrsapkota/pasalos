# Heal Particles

**Keywords:** Health, Plus, Green, Life, Regain.

**Description:**
Green "plus" signs (+) or glowing orbs floating upwards from a character, often spiraling.

## Usage

### 1. Canvas Particles
Standard particle emitter.
Sprite: `+` or soft glow.
Motion: Upward with sine wave sway `x += sin(y)`.
Color: `#00ff00` to `#ffffff`.
