# Sparkle Trail

**Keywords:** Glitter, Magic, Disney, Follow, Cursor.

**Description:**
A trail of 4-pointed stars that spawn at a moving point, shrink, rotate, and fade out.

## Usage

### 1. Canvas / CSS
Star shape: `✦`.
Rotation: Random.
Color: Gold, White, or Rainbow.
Blend Mode: `screen` or `overlay` for "shiny" look.
