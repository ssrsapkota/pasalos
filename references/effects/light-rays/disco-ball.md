# Disco Ball

**Keywords:** Reflection, Dots, Party, Glitter, Scatter.

**Description:**
Hundreds of small dots of light moving across surfaces, rotating around a center point.

## Usage

### 1. Canvas / Particles
Generate particle dots that move in elliptical paths or scroll across the screen.

### 2. CSS Blend Modes
A repeating tiling background of white dots with `mix-blend-mode: overlay` moving across a dark background.
