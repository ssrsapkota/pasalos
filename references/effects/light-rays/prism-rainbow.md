# Prism Rainbow

**Keywords:** Spectrum, Refraction, Pink Floyd, Dispersion.

**Description:**
White light splitting into its constituent colors (ROYGBIV).

## Usage

### 1. CSS Gradients
A linear gradient with the rainbow colors.

```css
background: linear-gradient(90deg, red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet);
```

### 2. WebGL Chromatic Aberration
Distort the Red, Green, and Blue channels differently based on distance from the center or a refraction index.
