# Countdown Leader

**Keywords:** Old, Film Start, Numbers, Circle, Rotate.

**Description:**
The classic 5..4..3..2..1 countdown with a rotating radar-sweep line and beeps.

## Usage

### 1. SVG Animation
Draw numbers and the sweeping circle sector.
Change number every second.
Add [Film Grain](film-grain.md) and [Reel Flicker](reel-flicker.md) for authenticity.
