# Film Burn

**Keywords:** Transition, Light Leak, Orange, Retro, Flash.

**Description:**
A transition where the "film" seems to catch fire or be overexposed, washing the screen in white/orange/red.

## Usage

### 1. CSS Overlay Animation
Animate a full-screen overlay's background color and opacity.
Keyframes:
0%: Transparent
40%: Orange `rgba(255, 100, 0, 0.5)`
50%: White `rgba(255, 255, 255, 1)` (Cut happens here)
60%: Orange
100%: Transparent
