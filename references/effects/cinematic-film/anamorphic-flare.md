# Anamorphic Flare

**Keywords:** JJ Abrams, Sci-Fi, Lens Flare, Blue Streak, Horizontal.

**Description:**
Long horizontal blue streaks of light originating from bright light sources.

## Usage

### 1. CSS Gradient & Blur
A wide, thin div with a blue gradient.
`background: linear-gradient(90deg, transparent, #0088ff, transparent);`
`filter: blur(5px);`
Put it behind bright UI elements.
