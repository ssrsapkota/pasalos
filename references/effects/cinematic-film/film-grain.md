# Film Grain

**Keywords:** Noise, Retro, Texture, Movie, Gritty.

**Description:**
A subtle layer of moving noise to reduce "digital perfectness" and add texture.

## Usage

### 1. SVG Filter (Turbulence)
Overlay an SVG `<feTurbulence>` filter with `type="fractalNoise"`.
Animate the `seed` or `transform` to make it alive.

### 2. Image Overlay
A semi-transparent noise GIF or heavy JPG.
`mix-blend-mode: overlay`.
