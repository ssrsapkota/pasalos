# Underwater Caustics

**Keywords:** Pool, Ocean, Light, Reflection, Shimmer.

**Description:**
[See light-rays/caustics.md](../light-rays/caustics.md).
Bright, web-like patterns of light on the floor of a body of water.

## Usage

### 1. WebGL/GLSL
Iterate a sine wave function to create a cellular pattern.

### 2. Alpha Mask Video
Overlay a looping video of real caustics with `mix-blend-mode: overlay`.
