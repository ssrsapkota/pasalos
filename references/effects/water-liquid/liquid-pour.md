# Liquid Pour

**Keywords:** Stream, Flow, Viscosity, Drink, Fill.

**Description:**
A continuous stream of liquid falling from a source, widening slightly at the bottom or piling up.

## Usage

### 1. CSS ScaleY
A long rectangle that scales down from `scaleY(0)` to `scaleY(1)` with `transform-origin: top`.

### 2. Canvas
A particle emitter that spawns many particles close together with high downward velocity, connected by a blur filter or "metaballs" thresholding.
