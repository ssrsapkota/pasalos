# Ice Crack

**Keywords:** Freeze, Break, Shatter, Fracture, Cold.

**Description:**
A sudden appearance of jagged lines radiating from a point, often with a "freezing" sound visual.

## Usage

### 1. SVG Stroke Animation
Draw jagged paths (`L x y L x y`) and animate `stroke-dashoffset`.

### 2. CSS Clip Path
Use multiple divs with clip-paths that start together and then separate slightly to simulate breaking.
