# Snowfall

**Keywords:** Winter, Cold, Flakes, Drift, Soft.

**Description:**
White particles drifting down slowly with horizontal sway.

## Usage

### 1. CSS Animation
A scrolling background image with `background-position` modification. Use at least 2 layers with different speeds for depth.

### 2. Particles.js
The classic "snow" preset.
- Opacity: Random
- Size: Random
- Move: Bottom, with random horizontal sway.
