# Ocean Foam

**Keywords:** Beach, Shore, Sea, White, Bubbles.

**Description:**
The white, churned-up water at the edge of a wave or shoreline.

## Usage

### 1. Fractal Noise
Multiple layers of noise textures moving at different speeds, masked by the wave shape.

### 2. Voronoi Pattern
A voronoi texture scrolling looks remarkably like sea foam.
