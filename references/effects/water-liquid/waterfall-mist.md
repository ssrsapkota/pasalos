# Waterfall Mist

**Keywords:** Cascade, Spray, Haze, Falling, Nature.

**Description:**
Water falling vertically, generating a cloud of mist at the bottom.

## Usage

### 1. Particle System
Emitter at the top sends particles down (water). Emitter at the bottom sends particles up and out (mist).

### 2. CSS Gradient Mask
A scrolling texture masked by a gradient to make it fade out at the bottom.
