# Frost Spread

**Keywords:** Freeze, Crystal, Ice, Window, Growth.

**Description:**
Ice crystals growing inward from the edges of the screen or an object.

## Usage

### 1. Image Sequence / Sprite
Hard to generate procedurally in CSS. Best to use a sequence of images showing progressive frost growth.

### 2. WebGL Reaction-Diffusion or Cellular Automata
Simulate crystal growth logic.
