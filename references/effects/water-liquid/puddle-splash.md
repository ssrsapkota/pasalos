# Puddle Splash

**Keywords:** Rain, Impact, Reflection, Wet Ground.

**Description:**
A ripple on the ground caused by a falling drop, often accompanied by small upward spray.

## Usage

### 1. Canvas / JS
Spawn a [Water Ripple](water-ripple.md) at the impact point `(x, y)`.

### 2. CSS 3D
Transform a ripple div `rotateX(60deg)` to make it lie flat on the "ground".
