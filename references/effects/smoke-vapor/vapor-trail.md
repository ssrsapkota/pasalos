# Vapor Trail

**Keywords:** Jet, Plane, Contrail, Sky, Path.

**Description:**
A long, thin white line left behind a fast-moving object, which slowly expands and dissipates into blobs.

## Usage

### 1. Particle Trail
Spawn high-density particles at the object's position.
Zero initial velocity (they stay where they were spawned).
Add slow turbulence/noise over time to make the straight line become wavy/broken.
