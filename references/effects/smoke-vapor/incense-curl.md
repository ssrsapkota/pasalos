# Incense Curl

**Keywords:** Calm, Smell, Thin, Laminar Flow, Turbulent.

**Description:**
A very thin, smooth line of smoke that rises straight up (laminar) then suddenly becomes chaotic and swirls (turbulent).

## Usage

### 1. Canvas / WebGL Fluid Sim
A high-resolution grid simulation is best.
Alternatively, use "curl noise" to perturb a line of particles.
Key characteristic: The transition from straight -> curly.
