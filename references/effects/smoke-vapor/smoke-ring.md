# Smoke Ring

**Keywords:** Torus, Vortex, O-shape, Puff, Trick.

**Description:**
A donut-shaped ring of smoke that travels forward while rotating internally.

## Usage

### 1. Canvas Particles (Torus)
Spawn particles in a circle `(r, theta)` moving forward `(vz)`.
The core visual trick is that the ring maintains its shape longer than a plume.
Fade out uniformly.
