# Exhaust Cloud

**Keywords:** Car, Truck, Pollution, Black, Puff.

**Description:**
Bursts of dark grey/black smoke emitted from a pipe, often in rhythm with an engine.

## Usage

### 1. Particle Pulses
Emit particles in bursts (e.g., every 0.5s).
Color: Dark Grey.
Spread: Medium.
Particles should slow down rapidly due to air resistance.
