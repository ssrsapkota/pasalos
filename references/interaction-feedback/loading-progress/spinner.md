# Spinner

**Description:**
A looping animation (usually circular) that indicates an indeterminate loading state. Used when the wait time is unknown.

**Key Principles:**
- **Indeterminate**: Use only when you don't know how long the process will take.
- **Minimalism**: Should be simple and not distract from the content that is loading.

**Common Patterns:**
- **SVG Circle**: A rotating SVG stroke.
- **Dual Ring**: Two rings rotating in opposite directions.
- **Dots**: Three dots pulsating.
