# Focus Ring

**Description:**
A visible outline or ring around an interactive element when it has keyboard focus. This is a critical accessibility requirement.

**Key Principles:**
- **Visibility**: The ring must be clearly visible against the background.
- **Separation**: Often useful to leave a small gap (offset) between the element and the focus ring to prevent it from blending into the element's border.
- **Customization**: While browser defaults exist, distinct custom focus rings often look better and align with branding.

**Common Patterns:**
- **Double Ring**: A white inner ring and a blue outer ring to ensure visibility on any background color.
- **Glow**: A soft, colored shadow that acts as a focus indicator.
- **Thick Border**: Simply thickening the existing border (careful not to shift layout).
