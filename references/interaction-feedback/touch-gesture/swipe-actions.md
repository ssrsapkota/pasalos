# Swipe Actions

**Description:**
Gestures involving moving a finger horizontally or vertically to trigger an action.

**Key Principles:**
- **Discoverability**: Hints (like a small peek or bounce) help users know swipe exists.
- **Threshold**: The action should only trigger after swiping a certain distance (e.g., 50%).

**Common Patterns:**
- **Swipe to Delete**: Common in email apps.
- **Carousel**: Swiping through images.
- **Drawer**: Swiping from the edge to open a menu.
