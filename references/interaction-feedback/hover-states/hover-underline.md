# Hover Underline

**Description:**
Animating or appearing decorations on text links during hover. This reinforces the interactive nature of text.

**Key Principles:**
- **Legibility**: Ensure the underline doesn't cross through descenders (letters like g, j, p, q, y).
- **Animation**: Animations should be quick. Left-to-right, center-out, or fading in are popular choices.
- **Color**: The underline can change color to add extra emphasis.

**Common Patterns:**
- **Fade In**: Underline opacity goes from 0 to 1.
- **Sweep**: Underline grows from left to right.
- **Thickness Change**: A thin line becomes thicker (e.g., 1px to 2px).
- **Distance**: The gap between text and underline decreases.
