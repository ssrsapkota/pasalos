# Time-based Color

**Keywords:** Circadian, sunrise, sunset, f.lux, night-shift.

**Description:**
Adjusting the color temperature or palette based on the time of day to match the user's environment.

## Usage

### 1. The Cycle
*   **Morning (6am - 12pm):** Bright, cool whites, energetic blues. (High Contrast).
*   **Afternoon (12pm - 5pm):** Warmer whites, standard contrast.
*   **Evening (5pm - 8pm):** Sunset hues, oranges/pinks, reduced blue light.
*   **Night (8pm+):** Dark mode, deep blues/blacks, red accents (to preserve night vision).

### 2. Blue Light Reduction
Removing the blue channel from the screen reduces eye strain and helps melatonin production.
*   *CSS:* `filter: sepia(30%)` or an overlay of transparent orange.

### 3. Application
*   Reader apps.
*   Smart Home interfaces.
