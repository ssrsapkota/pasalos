# Warm Palette

**Keywords:** Energy, passion, comfort, urgency, appetite.

**Description:**
Palettes dominated by red, orange, and yellow hues. These colors advance visual space (appear closer) and are stimulating to the viewer. They are often used for food, entertainment, and warnings.

## Usage

### 1. Associations
*   **Red:** Passion, danger, sales, love.
*   **Orange:** Creativity, youth, friendliness.
*   **Yellow:** Happiness, sunlight, caution.

### 2. Balancing
Warm colors can be overwhelming.
*   **Tip:** Mix with plenty of white space or cool neutrals (like a slate gray) to prevent visual fatigue.
*   **Accent:** A touch of Teal or Blue can make a warm palette pop without cooling it down effectively.

### 3. CSS Variables
```css
:root {
  --warm-bg: #fff5eb;
  --warm-primary: #e65100;
  --warm-secondary: #ffd600;
  --warm-text: #3e2723;
```
