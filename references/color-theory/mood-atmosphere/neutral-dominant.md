# Neutral Dominant

**Keywords:** Minimalism, modern, clean, expensive.

**Description:**
Palettes that rely almost exclusively on neutrals (white, gray, beige, black) with very little to no chromatic color. The visual interest comes from typography, spacing, and photography rather than color.

## Usage

### 1. The Power of Beige
"Greige" and warm neutrals are replacing sterile grays in modern UI.
*   **Sand/Cream:** Warm, organic feel.
*   **Slate/Charcoal:** Cool, technical feel.

### 2. Texture & Shadow
Since hue is absent, use elevation (shadows) and borders to define structure.
*   **1px Borders:** Essential for separating content areas in neutral designs.

### 3. Pop of Color
Strictly limit color to:
*   **Primary Action:** A single strong color (e.g., Black button or bright Red).
*   **Status Indicators:** Success/Error states.

```css
.card {
  background: #ffffff;
  border: 1px solid #e5e5e5;
  color: #171717;
}
```
