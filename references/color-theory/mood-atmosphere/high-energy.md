# High Energy

**Keywords:** Sport, gaming, youth, startup, loud.

**Description:**
Palettes that use high saturation and high brightness. Often involves neon colors, strong gradients, and dark backgrounds to make the colors pop.

## Usage

### 1. Saturation is Key
Avoid muted tones. Go for pure hues.
*   **Electric Blue:** #00FFFF
*   **Hot Pink:** #FF00FF
*   **Lime Green:** #00FF00

### 2. Dark Mode
High energy colors vibrate comfortably on dark backgrounds (Black or Dark Blue/Purple) but can be painful on white backgrounds.

### 3. Application
*   **Gaming UI:** Neons on black.
*   **Fitness App:** High contrast yellow/black.
*   **Marketing Landing Page:** Bold gradients.

**Warning:** Can cause eye strain if used for long-form reading backgrounds. Keep text high-contrast but readable.
