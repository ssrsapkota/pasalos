# Dark & Moody

**Keywords:** Luxury, cinema, mysterious, premium, immersive.

**Description:**
Palettes anchored in deep values—blacks, charcoal, midnight blues, deep forest greens. Unlike "Dark Mode" which is a utility, "Dark & Moody" is an aesthetic choice for emotional impact.

## Usage

### 1. It's Not Just Black
Avoid `#000000`. Use:
*   **Rich Black:** #0a0a0a
*   **Midnight:** #0f172a (Blue-based dark)
*   **Charcoal:** #1f1f1f

### 2. Glow and Reflection
Dark surfaces need light to define them.
*   Use subtle gradients to simulate lighting.
*   Use inner-shadows or "glassmorphism" borders to separate elements.

### 3. Accent Colors
Jewel tones work best here.
*   Gold, Emerald, Sapphire, Ruby.
*   Neon colors change the vibe from "Luxury" to "Cyberpunk".

### 4. Application
*   **Streaming Services:** Cinema mode.
*   **Luxury Cars/Watches:** Premium feel.
*   **Portfolios:** Focus on the work.
