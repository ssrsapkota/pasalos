# Cool Palette

**Keywords:** Calm, trust, professional, sterile, spacious.

**Description:**
Palettes dominated by blue, green, and purple hues. These colors recede in visual space (appear further away), creating a sense of depth and openness. They are the standard for tech, finance, and health.

## Usage

### 1. Associations
*   **Blue:** Trust, technology, corporate, water.
*   **Green:** Growth, money, nature, safety.
*   **Purple:** Luxury, mystery, spiritual.

### 2. Temperature Check
Not all "cool" colors feel cold.
*   **Warm Cool:** Turquoise (Green-Blue with yellow) feels tropical.
*   **Cold Cool:** Ice Blue (Blue with white/gray) feels sterile.

### 3. Application
Ideal for productivity tools where you want the user to remain focused and calm for long periods.

```css
:root {
  --cool-bg: #f0f9ff;
  --cool-surface: #ffffff;
  --cool-primary: #0ea5e9;
  --cool-text: #0f172a;
}
```
