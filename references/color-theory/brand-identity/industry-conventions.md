# Industry Conventions

**Keywords:** Expected-patterns, safe-bets, vertical-specifics.

**Description:**
Certain industries have strongly established color conventions. Breaking these can be innovative, but it can also confuse users or destroy trust.

## Usage

### 1. Finance (Fintech)
*   **Color:** Royal Blue or Dark Green.
*   **Why:** Stability, Trust, Money.
*   *Avoid:* Hot Pink (feels risky).

### 2. Healthcare & Eco
*   **Color:** Teal, Mint, Sage Green, Light Blue.
*   **Why:** Cleanliness, Health, Nature, Serenity.
*   *Avoid:* Dark/Muddy Brown (feels dirty).

### 3. Food & Beverage
*   **Color:** Red, Orange, Yellow.
*   **Why:** Appetite simulation.
*   *Avoid:* Cool Blue (suppresses appetite).

### 4. Luxury
*   **Color:** Black, Gold, Silver.
*   **Why:** Exclusivity, Drama.
*   *Avoid:* Rainbow colors (feels cheap).

### 5. Tech / SaaS
*   **Color:** Indigo, Purple, Electric Blue.
*   **Why:** Innovation, Digital, Future.
