# 60-30-10 Rule

**Keywords:** Distribution, balance, interior-design, golden-ratio.

**Description:**
A classic interior design rule applied to UI. It ensures a balanced application of color that is neither boring nor chaotic.

## Usage

### 1. The Breakdown
*   **60% Neutral:** The background color. White, Light Gray, Cream, or Dark Gray (in dark mode). Ideally plenty of whitespace.
*   **30% Secondary:** The structure color. Cards, headers, footers, secondary buttons, subtle illustrations.
*   **10% Accent:** The action color. Primary buttons, CTAs, links, meaningful icons.

### 2. Why it works
It forces hierarchy. The user knows that the "10%" color is the interactive/important one because it is scarce.

### 3. Violation?
You can break it, but usually by increasing whitespace (more neutral) rather than increasing accent. A 80-15-5 ratio is even more elegant.
