# Pastel & Soft

**Keywords:** Cute, friendly, accessible, gen-z, approachable.

**Description:**
Colors with high lightness and low-to-medium saturation. They feel "soft" to the touch.

## Usage

### 1. Key Colors
*   **Baby Blue:** `#89CFF0`
*   **Millennial Pink:** `#F3CFC6`
*   **Mint:** `#98FF98`
*   **Lavender:** `#E6E6FA`

### 2. The Black Problem
Mismatched Contrast.
*   **Bad:** Pure black text on pastel feels too harsh.
*   **Good:** Dark Slate or Deep Colored Ink (e.g., Deep Purple text on Lavender background) looks much more harmonious.

### 3. Application
*   SaaS dashboards (to reduce eye strain), Baby products, Candy/Desserts.
