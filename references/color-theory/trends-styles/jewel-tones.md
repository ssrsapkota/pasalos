# Jewel Tones

**Keywords:** Rich, saturated, deep, expensive, dark-mode.

**Description:**
Colors resembling gemstones (Emerald, Sapphire, Ruby, Amethyst). They are highly saturated but have a medium-to-dark value, making them feel extremely premium.

## Usage

### 1. Key Colors
*   **Emerald Green:** `#50C878` (or deeper `#046307`)
*   **Sapphire Blue:** `#0F52BA`
*   **Ruby Red:** `#E0115F`
*   **Amethyst Purple:** `#9966CC`

### 2. Pairing
They work exceptionally well with:
*   **Gold/Silver/Metallic** accents.
*   **Deep Black/Charcoal** backgrounds.

### 3. Application
*   Luxury goods, Fintech VIP tiers, Awards & Recognition.
