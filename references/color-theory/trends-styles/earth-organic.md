# Earth & Organic

**Keywords:** Sustainability, nature, human, grounded, pottery.

**Description:**
Colors derived from soil, plants, and natural light. These colors feel "unprocessed" and honest.

## Usage

### 1. Key Colors
*   **Terracotta:** `#cc8866`
*   **Sage Green:** `#8a9a5b`
*   **Ochre:** `#d6a83e`
*   **Cream/Off-White:** `#f5f5dc` (Never pure white)

### 2. Texture Association
These colors pair extremely well with paper textures, grain, or serif typography.

### 3. Application
*   Coffee shops, Sustainable Fashion, Ceramic/Art studios, Wellness apps.
