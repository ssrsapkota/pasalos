# Gradient Direction

**Keywords:** Light-source, flow, energy, angle.

**Description:**
The angle of a gradient tells a story about the light source and energy.

## Usage

### 1. Top-Down (180deg)
*   **Feel:** Natural, sky-to-ground, stable.
*   *Use:* Backgrounds, buttons (makes them look convex).

### 2. Diagonal (135deg)
*   **Feel:** Dynamic, forward-moving, energetic.
*   *Use:* Brand backgrounds, cards.

### 3. Left-to-Right (90deg)
*   **Feel:** Progress, reading direction, speed.
*   *Use:* Progress bars.

### 4. Color Stops
Don't use just 0% and 100%.
*   Add a midpoint at 50% or non-linear easing to make the gradient look "richer" and less mechanical.
