# Background & Foreground

**Keywords:** Layering, depth, separation, container.

**Description:**
The fundamental relationship of UI design. Defining what is the "canvas" and what is the "content".

## Usage

### 1. Off-White
Pure white (`#FFFFFF`) can be harsh on monitors.
*   **Technique:** Use a very subtle "warm" or "cool" off-white for the main background (e.g., `#FAFAFA`) and keep pure white for the "Cards" or content containers.

### 2. The Card Metaphor
*   **Background:** Darker (Light Gray).
*   **Foreground (Card):** Lighter (White) + Shadow.
*   *Result:* The card feels like it is floating above the background.

### 3. Inverted (Dark Mode)
*   **Background:** Darker (Black).
*   **Foreground (Card):** Lighter (Dark Gray).
