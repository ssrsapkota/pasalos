# Cultural Meaning

**Keywords:** Internationalization, localization, risk, taboo.

**Description:**
Color meanings are not universal. They change drastically across borders.

## Usage

### 1. White
*   **West:** Purity, Weddings.
*   **East (China/Korea):** Death, Mourning.

### 2. Red
*   **West:** Danger, Love, Stop.
*   **East (China):** Luck, Prosperity, Celebration, Money.
*   *Stock Market:* Red means "Up/Good" in China, but "Down/Loss" in the West.

### 3. Green
*   **West:** Money, Go, Nature.
*   **Middle East:** Islam, Strength, Fertility.
*   **South America:** Death (in some jungle cultures).

### 4. Application
Check your target audience. If building a global app, neutral colors (Blue/Grayscale) are the safest bet.
