# Color Emotion Map

**Keywords:** Psychology, marketing, feeling, brain, subconscious.

**Description:**
The general emotional response elicited by specific hues in a western context.

## Usage

### 1. The Breakdown
*   **Red:** Excitement, Love, Danger, Urgent, Hot.
*   **Orange:** Playful, Friendly, Cheap, Social, Loud.
*   **Yellow:** Happy, Optimistic, Caution, Anxious.
*   **Green:** Nature, Growth, Money, Safe, Fresh.
*   **Blue:** Trust, Calm, Corporate, Cold, Stable.
*   **Purple:** Creative, Royal, Spiritual, Mysterious.
*   **Pink:** Feminine, Nurturing, Sweet, Romantic.
*   **Black:** Luxury, Power, Grief, Modern.
*   **White:** Purity, Clean, Sterile, Empty.

### 2. Context Matters
Red means "Love" on Valentine's day, but "ERROR" on a computer screen. Context always overrides hue.
