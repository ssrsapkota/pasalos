# Color Rhythm

**Keywords:** Flow, repetition, pattern, consistency.

**Description:**
Repeating colors across a layout to create a sense of movement and connection.

## Usage

### 1. Leading the Eye
If you use a specific "GOLD" color for your "Next" buttons, using that same Gold for progress bars or arrow icons creates a visual path.
*   The eye naturally jumps between areas of matching color.

### 2. The Rule of Repetition
A single splash of color looks like a mistake.
*   **Rule:** If you introduce a strong color, repeat it at least once elsewhere (even in a small dot or line) to tie the composition together.

### 3. Alternating Rhythm
*   Backgrounds: White -> Light Gray -> White -> Light Gray.
*   This separates content sections clearly without needing heavy borders.
