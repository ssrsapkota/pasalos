# Tetradic (Double Complementary)

**Keywords:** Rich, complex, varied, challenging.

**Description:**
Uses four colors arranged into two complementary pairs. This rich color scheme offers plenty of possibilities for variation but is the hardest to balance.

## Usage

### 1. The Trap
If you use all four colors in equal amounts, the design may settle into a state of chaos.
*   **Rule:** Choose one dominant color. The other three should be used for hierarchy (text, subordinate actions, borders).

### 2. Rectangular vs Square
*   **Rectangle:** Interaction between two adjacent pairs and their complements.
*   **Square:** Four evenly spaced colors.

### 3. Application
Best used in complex data visualizations or content-heavy sites where many distinct types of information need to be color-coded without sharing a hue.

**Tip:** Pay attention to the balance between warm and cool colors in your design.
