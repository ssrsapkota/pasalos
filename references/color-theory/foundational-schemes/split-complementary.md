# Split Complementary

**Keywords:** Balanced, vibrant, safer-contrast.

**Description:**
A variation of the complementary color scheme. In addition to the base color, it uses the two colors adjacent to its complement. This scheme offers the same high contrast as the complementary scheme but with less tension. It is often a better choice for beginners because it is difficult to mess up.

## Usage

### 1. Composition
*   **Base:** Main Brand Color.
*   **Accents:** Two colors on either side of the direct opposite.

### 2. Why use it?
It retains the excitement of high contrast but feels more "nuanced" and flexible than a strict binary output. It allows for a primary action color and a secondary "alert" or "highlight" color that are distinct but related.

### 3. Example
*   **Base:** Blue.
*   **Complement:** Orange.
*   **Split Complements:** Yellow-Orange and Red-Orange.
*   *Application:* Blue background, Yellow-Orange for primary buttons, Red-Orange for "Hot" tags or alerts.
