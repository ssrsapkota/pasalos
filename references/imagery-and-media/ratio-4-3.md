# 4:3 Aspect Ratio

> **Traditional photo ratio**

A bit taller than 16:9. Feels more "classic" and less "cinematic".

## Usage
-   **Product Cards:** Offers more vertical room for the product than 16:9.
-   **Gallery Grids:** A good balance between square and wide.
-   **Retro Media:** Representing older TV or photo formats.

## Calculation
-   `height = width * 0.75`
-   CSS: `aspect-ratio: 4 / 3;`
