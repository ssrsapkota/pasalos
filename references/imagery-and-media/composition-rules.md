# Composition Rules

> **Rule of thirds, focal points**

Good composition leads the eye.

## Rule of Thirds
-   Place the subject at the intersection of grid lines (1/3 in).
-   leaving "negative space" (open sky, wall) for text overlays.

## Focal Points
-   Ensure the image has one clear subject.
-   Avoid busy backgrounds where text will be placed.

## Direction
-   Lines in the image (roads, pointing fingers, gaze) should point *towards* the UI content, not away from it.
