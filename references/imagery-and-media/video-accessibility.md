# Video Accessibility

> **Captions and transcripts**

Video must be accessible to users who are deaf, hard of hearing, or in a sound-off environment.

## Requirements
-   **Captions (CC):** Synchronized text overlay. Required for WCAG AA.
-   **Transcripts:** A text version of the dialogue/content available below the video (great for SEO too).
-   **Audio Description:** Describing visual action for blind users (AAA requirement, but good practice).
-   **No Flashing:** Ensure video doesn't flash more than 3 times a second (seizure risk).
