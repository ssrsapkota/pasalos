# Hero Illustrations

> **Large feature artwork**

The centerpiece of a landing page or section.

## Role
-   **Storytelling:** Explains the product narrative at a glance.
-   **Vibe Setter:** Establishes the brand personality immediately.

## Placement
-   Usually right-aligned (on desktop) to balance the left-aligned value proposition text.
-   Should not interfere with the H1 or CTA button readability.
