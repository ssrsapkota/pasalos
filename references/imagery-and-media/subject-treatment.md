# Subject Treatment

> **People, products, abstract**

How subjects are framed and presented.

## People
-   **Eye Contact:** Looking at the camera creates connection. Looking at the content directs user attention.
-   **Candid:** "Caught in the moment" feels more authentic than posed handshakes.

## Products
-   **Context:** Show properties in use (lifestyle) vs. isolated on white (e-commerce).
-   **Macro:** Close-ups to show texture and quality.

## Abstract
-   Use for backgrounds or when specific representation isn't needed.
-   Avoid busy patterns that compromise text readability.
