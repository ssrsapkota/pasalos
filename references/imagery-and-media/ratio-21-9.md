# 21:9 Aspect Ratio

> **Ultrawide cinematic**

Extremely wide and short. "Panavision" style.

## Usage
-   **Panoramic banners:** decorative strips of imagery.
-   **Immersive Gaming/Movie UI:** mimicking theater screens.
-   **Space-saving:** Adding visual flavor without pushing content too far down the page.

## Calculation
-   `height = width * 0.428...`
-   CSS: `aspect-ratio: 21 / 9;`
