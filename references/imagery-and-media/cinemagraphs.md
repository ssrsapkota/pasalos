# Cinemagraphs

> **Still image with subtle motion**

A largely static photo with a small repeating movement (e.g., steam rising from coffee, leaves rustling).

## Effect
-   Hypnotic and high-quality feel.
-   Less distracting than full video.
-   Lower file size than full video.

## Format
-   High-quality looping MP4/WebM is usually better than GIF (smaller size, more colors).
