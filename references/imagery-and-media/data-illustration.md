# Data Illustration

> **Charts and infographics**

making data beautiful and understandable.

## Principles
-   **Clarity first:** Don't sacrifice readability for style.
-   **Color Coding:** Use color meaningfully (Green = Good, Red = Bad), not just decoratively.
-   **Simplicity:** Remove "chart junk" (unnecessary grid lines, 3D effects).

## Types
-   **Donut/Pie:** Parts of a whole.
-   **Bar:** Comparison.
-   **Line:** Trend over time.
