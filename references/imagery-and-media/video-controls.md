# Video Controls

> **Custom player UI**

Standard browser controls often clash with the UI design.

## Essentials
-   **Play/Pause:** Huge center button for initial play, smaller corner button for controls.
-   **Progress Bar:** Scrubber to jump through time.
-   **Volume:** Mute toggle.
-   **Fullscreen:** Expand to immersive view.

## Style
-   Overlay controls on hover.
-   Keep them translucent so they don't block the content.
