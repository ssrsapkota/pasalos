# Image Diversity

> **Inclusive representation**

Representation matters. Your imagery should reflect the diversity of your user base.

## Aspects
-   **Race & Ethnicity:** Include a balance of backgrounds.
-   **Gender:** Avoid stereotypes (e.g., men in leadership only, women in support only).
-   **Age:** Include different age groups, not just 20-somethings.
-   **Ability:** Show people with disabilities using technology naturally.
-   **Body Type:** Represent various body types.

## Intent
-   Diversity should feel natural, not a "checklist" in a single photo.
-   It's better to having diversity across a *set* of photos than trying to cram everyone into one "We Are The World" frame.
