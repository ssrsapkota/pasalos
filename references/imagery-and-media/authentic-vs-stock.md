# Authentic vs Stock

> **Real vs generic imagery**

Generic stock photos decrease trust. Authentic imagery increases it.

## The "Stock" Look (Avoid)
-   Overlit studio lighting.
-   Unnatural diversity (the "perfect" team).
-   Generic handshakes, "pointing at whiteboard".

## The Authentic Look (Embrace)
-   Natural lighting.
-   Imperfect environments (real offices, real desks).
-   Genuine expressions (not frozen smiles).
-   If you must use stock, choose "editorial" style collections (Unsplash, Pexels) over "corporate" stock.
