# Image Style

> **Photography direction and mood**

Defining the overall "vibe" of photography ensures it aligns with brand personality.

## Dimensions
-   **Warm vs Cool:** Cozy/inviting vs. Professional/tech.
-   **Bright vs Dark:** High key (light, airy) vs. Low key (dramatic, serious).
-   **Natural vs Staged:** Candid look vs. Studio perfection.

## Consistency
-   Images from different sources (stock, proprietary) should look like they belong to the same family.
-   Use color grading or overlays to unify disparate sources.
