# 3:2 Aspect Ratio

> **Classic photography**

The native aspect ratio of 35mm film and most DSLRs.

## Usage
-   **Photography Portfolios:** Respects the original crop of most photos.
-   **Blog Featured Images:** A nice middle ground that isn't too short (16:9) or too tall.

## Calculation
-   `height = width * 0.666...`
-   CSS: `aspect-ratio: 3 / 2;`
