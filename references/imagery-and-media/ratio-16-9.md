# 16:9 Aspect Ratio

> **Widescreen video standard**

The standard for video, monitors, and most hero imagery.

## Usage
-   **Hero Headers:** great for immersive, cinematic intros.
-   **Video Players:** The default container size for YouTube/Vimeo embeds.
-   **Card Media:** often too short for cards with lots of text, but great for media-focused cards.

## Calculation
-   `height = width * 0.5625`
-   CSS: `aspect-ratio: 16 / 9;`
