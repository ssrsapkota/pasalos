# Autoplay Rules

> **When autoplay is appropriate**

Autoplay can be annoying and user-hostile if misused.

## Acceptable
-   **Background Heros:** Muted, decorative.
-   **Social Feeds:** Muted, plays as you scroll (TikTok/Instagram style).
-   **Hover Previews:** Playing a short snippet when hovering a card (Netflix style).

## Unacceptable
-   **Sound On:** NEVER autoplay sound.
-   **Mobile Data:** Be mindful of autoplaying heavy videos on cellular connections.
