# Space Contrast

**Description:**
Using whitespace (negative space) to create contrast and hierarchy.

**Key Principles:**
- **Density**: Denser areas feel related; sparse areas feel more important or luxurious.
- **Breathing Room**: Give important elements more space to stand out.

**Common Patterns:**
- **Margins**: Increasing margins around a CTA to isolate it.
- **Section Breaks**: Large vertical gaps between distinct sections.
