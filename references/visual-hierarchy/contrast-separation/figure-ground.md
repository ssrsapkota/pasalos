# Figure-Ground

**Description:**
The ability to distinguish an object (figure) from its surroundings (ground).

**Key Principles:**
- **Clarity**: The figure should be clearly separated from the ground to avoid visual confusion.
- **Ambiguity**: Avoid ambiguous backgrounds that compete with the foreground content.

**Common Patterns:**
- **White on Grey**: A white card on a light grey background creates a clear figure-ground relationship.
- **Dark Mode**: Light text (figure) on dark background (ground).
