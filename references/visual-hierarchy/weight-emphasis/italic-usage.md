# Italic Usage

**Description:**
Slanted text used for emphasis, differentiation, or specific semantic meaning (like titles of works).

**Key Principles:**
- **Readability**: Italics are harder to read in long blocks. Use sparingly.
- **Voice**: Can imply a shift in tone or an internal thought.

**Common Patterns:**
- **Captions**: Often italicized below images.
- **Quotes**: Blockquotes may use italics.
- **Foreign Words**: Standard practice for foreign terms.
