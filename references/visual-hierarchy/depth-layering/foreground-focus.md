# Foreground Focus

**Description:**
Techniques to ensure the foreground element pops out, even without standard shadows.

**Key Principles:**
- **Detail**: The foreground should have more detail/contrast than the background.
- **Sharpness**: Backgrounds can be slightly blurred (depth of field effect) to emphasize the sharp foreground.

**Common Patterns:**
- **Hero Header**: Sharp text over a slightly blurred or darkened hero image.
