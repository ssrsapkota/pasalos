# Above the Fold

**Description:**
The portion of the webpage visible without scrolling.

**Key Principles:**
- **Hook**: You must hook the user's interest immediately here.
- **CTA**: There should usually be a clear action available without scrolling.

**Common Patterns:**
- **Hero Section**: Large headline, subheadline, and primary button.
- **Value Prop**: Clear statement of what the site/app does.
