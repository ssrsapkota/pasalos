# F-Pattern

**Description:**
The natural eye-scanning pattern for text-heavy content. Users scan the top, then down a bit and across, then down the left side.

**Key Principles:**
- **Key Info**: Place important information across the top and down the left edge.
- **Headlines**: Use headlines to catch the "across" scans.

**Common Patterns:**
- **Blog Posts**: Classic example of F-pattern scanning.
- **Search Results**: Scanning titles down the list.
