# Style Grouping

**Description:**
Items that look alike (share the same shape, size, styling) are perceived as being related or of equal importance.

**Key Principles:**
- **Similarity**: Gestalt principle of similarity.
- **Function**: Users expect similar-looking elements to function similarly.

**Common Patterns:**
- **Navigation Links**: All look the same.
- **Primary Buttons**: All primary buttons on the site share the same style.
