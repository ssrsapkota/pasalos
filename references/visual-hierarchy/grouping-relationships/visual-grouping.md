# Visual Grouping

**Description:**
The Gestalt principle of proximity: items that are close together are perceived as a group.

**Key Principles:**
- **Proximity**: The strongest grouping signal. Put related items closer to each other than to unrelated items.
- **Regions**: Breaking content into distinct regions helps users process information.

**Common Patterns:**
- **Cards**: A headline, image, and button grouped in a card.
- **Form Sections**: "Personal Info" fields grouped together, separated from "Billing Info".
