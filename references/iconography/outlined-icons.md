# Outlined Icons

> **Stroke-only, light and airy**

Outlined icons rely on strokes (lines) to define shapes, creating a lightweight, modern, and clean aesthetic. They are versatile and work well in dense UIs where visual weight needs to be managed.

## Characteristics
-   **Stroke Width:** Consistent thickness (e.g., 1.5px or 2px).
-   **Open Shapes:** Forms are not filled, allowing background colors to show through.
-   **detail:** Often simplified to ensure legibility at small sizes.
-   **Corner Style:** usually matches the brand (sharp, rounded, or soft).

## Usage
-   **Navigation:** Tab bars, sidebars.
-   **Action Buttons:** Edit, delete, settings (when secondary).
-   **Data Display:** Tables, lists where visual noise must be minimized.

## Best Practices
-   Ensure stroke width is sufficient for visibility on all backgrounds.
-   Use rounded caps/joins for a friendlier look, or mitered for a sharper, technical feel.
-   Maintain a consistent stroke weight across the entire set.
