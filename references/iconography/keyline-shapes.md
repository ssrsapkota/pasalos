# Keyline Shapes

> **Circle, square, rectangle templates**

Keylines are underlying templates within the icon grid that guide the sizing of basic shapes to maintain optical consistency.

## The Shapes
-   **Square:** The basic compact block.
-   **Circle:** The maximal curved shape.
-   **Vertical Rectangle:** For tall, thin objects (smartphone, document).
-   **Horizontal Rectangle:** For wide, short objects (credit card, landscape).

## Usage
-   Draw your icons *within* these keyline boundaries.
-   If a square icon feels too heavy next to a circular icon, shrink the square slightly or grow the circle slightly until they feel balanced.
-   Keylines are guides, not strict walls. Optical adjustments (overshoot) often require breaking the keyline.
