# Isometric Icons

> **3D perspective icons**

Isometric icons are drawn using an isometric grid (typically 30-degree angles), giving them a 3D appearance without vanishing points.

## Characteristics
-   **3D Volume:** Shows top, side, and front views simultaneously.
-   **Geometric:** Built on a strict grid.
-   **Lighting:** visualizes light source and shadows to enhance form.

## Usage
-   **Marketing:** Hero sections, feature explanations.
-   **Complex Concepts:** visualizing infrastructure, hardware, or multi-step processes.
-   **Enterprise Software:**  Representing databases, servers, and networks.

## Best Practices
-   Maintain consistent lighting direction across all icons.
-   Keep the level of detail appropriate for the size; too much detail gets muddy at small sizes.
-   Use a consistent color palette for sides vs. tops to reinforce the 3D effect.
