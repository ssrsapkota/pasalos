# Hand-Drawn Icons

> **Organic, playful, imperfect**

Hand-drawn icons emulate the look of sketches or doodles. They bring a human, approachable, and creative feel to a design.

## Characteristics
-   **Irregular Lines:** Wobbly or variable-width strokes.
-   **Imperfect Shapes:** Circles aren't perfect, lines aren't perfectly straight.
-   **Texture:** May include rough edges or sketch marks.

## Usage
-   **Playful Brands:**  Children's products, creative tools, lifestyle brands.
-   **Storytelling:**  Marketing pages, personalized experiences.
-   **Emphasis on "Human":**  Services that want to feel personal and less corporate.

## Best Practices
-   Don't overdo the messiness; legibility is still key.
-   Ensure the style matches the typography (e.g., pair with a handwritten or rounded font).
-   Use sparingly in strict functional areas (like complex data tables) where clarity is paramount.
