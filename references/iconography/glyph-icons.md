# Glyph Icons

> **Simplified symbolic forms**

Glyph icons are highly simplified, symbolic representations of concepts. They prioritize instant recognition over realistic detail.

## Characteristics
-   **Reductionist:** Minimal detail, focuses on the core concept.
-   **Monochrome:** Usually single color.
-   **Symbolic:** Uses established conventions (e.g., magnifying glass for search).

## Usage
-   **System UI:** Operating systems, file managers.
-   **Small Sizes:** Extremely effective at 16x16px or smaller.
-   **Utilities:** Toolbars, context menus.

## Best Practices
-   Avoid unnecessary decoration.
-   Test at very small sizes (e.g., 12px, 16px) to ensure clarity.
-   Stick to universal metaphors for maximum understanding.
