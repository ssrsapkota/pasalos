# Learned Icons

> **Platform-specific conventions**

Learned icons are symbols that users have come to understand through repeated exposure on specific platforms (iOS, Android, etc.) or within specific domains.

## Examples
-   **Hamburger Menu (Three lines):** Menu. (Widely understood now, but was originally learned).
-   **Kebab Menu (Three dots):** More options.
-   **Share sheets:** The "box with arrow up" (iOS) vs "three connected dots" (Android).

## Risks
-   New users might not understand them immediately.
-   Context is crucial.
-   Ideally, pair with tooltips or labels until confidence in user understanding is high.
