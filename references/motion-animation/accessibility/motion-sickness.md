# Motion Sickness

Avoiding vestibular triggers that cause physical discomfort.

### Triggers
- **Large Area Movement**: Parallax or scroll-jacking where large parts of the screen move at different speeds.
- **Scale/Zoom**: Rapid "flying through" effects.
- **Disorientation**: Spinning or rotation (especially 3D).
### Mitigation
- Keep animations localized (small UI elements).
- Follow 1:1 interaction physics.
- Provide a way to turn it off.
