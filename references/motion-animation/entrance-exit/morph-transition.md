# Morph Transition

Seamlessly transforming one element into another.

### Mechanics
- **Technique**: Often called "Shared Element Transition" or FLIP (First, Last, Invert, Play).
- **Use Case**:
    - Floating Action Button expanding into a full screen sheet.
    - Thumbnail image expanding to full width detail view.
- **Goal**: Maintaining continuity of context. The user knows exactly where the new view came from.
