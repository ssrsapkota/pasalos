# Slide In

Movement from off-screen or from a concealed position.

### Mechanics
- **Property**: `transform: translate(x, y)`.
- **Direction**:
    - **Up**: Optimistic, uplifting (common for lists).
    - **Down**: Grounding.
    - **Left/Right**: Navigation context (forward/backward).
- **Distance**: Keep it short (e.g., 20px) for subtle entrances, or full screen for drawers.
