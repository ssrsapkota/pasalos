# Duration Scale

Standardized durations ensure consistency across the application.

### Instant (100ms)
- Used for: Hover states, button presses, micro-feedbacks.
- Perception: Feels immediate, snappy.

### Fast (200ms)
- Used for: Small transitions, dropdowns, tooltips appearing.
- Perception: Quick but noticeable.

### Normal (300ms)
- Used for: Modal dialogs, drawer slides, most standard UI animations.
- Perception: Smooth, natural speed for most interactions.

### Slow (500ms+)
- Used for: Large page transitions, complex illustrations, decorative motion.
- Perception: Deliberate, emphasizes the movement.
