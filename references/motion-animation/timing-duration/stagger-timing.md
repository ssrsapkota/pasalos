# Stagger Timing

Creating rhythm by offsetting start times of group elements.

### Sequential Delays
- **Technique**: Delay each subsequent item by a small amount (e.g., 50ms - 100ms).
- **Use Case**: Lists, grids, card collections loading in.
- **Benefit**: Reduces visual overwhelm and guides the eye through the content.
- **Limit**: Cap the total duration or number of staggered items to prevent long wait times.
