# Slide Panel

## Off-Canvas Navigation
Panels that slide in to cover or push content, often used for mobile navigation or supplementary details.

### Directions
- **Left**: Primary navigation. Standard for mobile drawers.
- **Right**: Secondary actions, filters, help, or item details.
- **Top/Bottom**: Notifications or quick settings (less common).

### Behavior Types
1.  **Overlay**: Slides *over* the main content. Best for temporary focus (mobile nav).
2.  **Push**: Pushes the main content to the side. Keeps context visible but shrinks available space.
3.  **Reveal**: Main content slides away to reveal the panel underneath (rare, "parallax" effect).

## Usage
- Ensure a visible "Close" button or click-outside behavior.
- On Mobile: Often takes up 80-100% of the width.
- On Desktop: Often fixed width (e.g., 400px) or percentage (30%).
