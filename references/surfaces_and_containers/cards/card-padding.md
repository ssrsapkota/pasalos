# Card Padding

## Internal Spacing
Padding creates breathing room and prevents content from feeling cramped.

### Strategies
- **Uniform**: Equal padding on all sides (e.g., `p-6`). Simple and effective.
- **Variable**: Less padding for headers/footers to allow full-bleed styling or tighter grouping, more padding for the body content.
- **Responsive**: Reduce padding on mobile devices to conserve screen real estate (e.g., `p-4` mobile, `p-6` or `p-8` desktop).

### Guidelines
- **Density**:
  - *Compact*: 8px-12px (`p-2`, `p-3`). For data-heavy interfaces.
  - *Standard*: 16px-24px (`p-4`, `p-6`). Good for most content cards.
  - *Spacious*: 32px+ (`p-8`). For marketing features or hero cards.

## Alignment
- Ensure content within the card aligns consistently with the padding edges.
- Use negative margins (in moderation) if you need an element (like a header image) to ignore the padding and span the full width.
