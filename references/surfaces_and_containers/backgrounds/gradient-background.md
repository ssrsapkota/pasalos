# Gradient Background

## Color Transitions
Adding depth and interest.

### Types
- **Linear**: Directional flow (e.g., `bg-gradient-to-r`). Good for buttons or headers.
- **Radial**: Focused spotlight effect. Good for hero sections.
- **Conic**: Angular sweep. Good for pies charts or loading spinners.

### Subtlety
- **Mesh Gradients**: Multiple blurred color blobs merging together.
- **Glass**: Semi-transparent gradient over a blurred backdrop.

### Text Contrast
- Ensure text remains readable over the entire gradient range.
- Use text-shadow or a "scrim" (semi-transparent overlay) if necessary.
