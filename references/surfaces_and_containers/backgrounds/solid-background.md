# Solid Background

## Flat Color Fills
The foundation of most UI surfaces.

### Usage
- **Base Layer**: typically white (light mode) or very dark gray/black (dark mode).
- **Surface Layer**: Slightly distinct color to indicate a raised surface (e.g., `gray-50`).
- **Accent**: Brand colors used for buttons, badges, or headers.

### Consistency
- Use a limited palette of neutrals.
- Avoid pure black (`#000000`) for large areas; it can cause eye strain and smear on OLED screens. Use `#050505` or similar.
