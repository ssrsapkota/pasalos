# Wave Divider

## Curved Section Breaks
Organic transitions between background colors.

### Implementation
- **SVG**: Use an SVG shape (path) positioned absolutely at the bottom or top of a section.
- **Aspect Ratio**: maintain the curve's aspect ratio to prevent distortion on wide screens. `preserveAspectRatio="none"` allows stretching if desired.
- **Fill**: The fill color must match the adjacent section's background color exactly.

### Variations
- **Sinusoidal**: Smooth, gentle waves.
- **Spiky**: jagged peaks for energetic brands.
- **Layered**: Multiple waves with different opacities creating depth.
