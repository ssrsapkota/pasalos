# Decorative Divider

## Ornamental Separators
Adds personality and brand flair to the page break.

### Types
- **Icons**: A small icon or logo mark centered between sections.
- **Glyphs**: Typography characters (~ * •) used as a divider.
- **Shapes**: Geometric patterns or SVG shapes.

### Styling
- **Color**: Often uses brand accent colors.
- **Size**: Should generally be smaller/lighter than the main content to avoid distraction.
- **Alignment**: Centered usually works best.
