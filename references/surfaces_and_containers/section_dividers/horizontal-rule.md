# Horizontal Rule

## Simple Line Separator
The most basic way to divide content.

### Styles
- **Solid**: Standard `border-t` or `<hr />`.
- **Dashed/Dotted**: Less visual weight, indicates a looser connection between sections.
- **Faded**: Uses opacity or gradients (transparent -> opaque -> transparent) to fade out at the edges.

### Usage
- **Full Width**: Separates major sections.
- **Inset**: Has margins on left/right to indicate it belongs to a contained element (like a card).
- **With Text**: "OR" or a chapter title centered in the middle of the line.
