# Border Style

## Line Characteristics
Variations beyond the solid line.

### Types
- **Solid**: The default. Reliable, structural.
- **Dashed**: Typically used for "drop zones" (file uploads) or temporary/placeholder items.
- **Dotted**: Used for less critical dividers or data visualizations. often hard to see at 1px width.
- **Double**: Old-school style, rarely used in modern UI unless for a specific retro aesthetic.

### CSS
- `border-style: solid;`
- `border-style: dashed;`
- `border-style: dotted;`
