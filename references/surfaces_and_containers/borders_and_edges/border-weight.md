# Border Weight

## Thickness & Hierarchy
Stroke width communicates importance and stability.

### Weights
- **Hairline (1px)**: Standard for dividers, inputs, and card boundaries. subtle and precise.
- **Medium (2px)**: Used for active states (focus rings), selected items, or buttons requiring more emphasis.
- **Thick (3px-4px)**: Stylistic choices, brutalist designs, or very prioritized focused states.
- **Heavy (8px+)**: Almost exclusively for decorative purposes or very specific brutalist/neo-brutalist styles.

### Consistency
- Maintain a consistent base border width (usually 1px).
- Use thicker borders sparingly to denote state changes (hover, focus, active).
