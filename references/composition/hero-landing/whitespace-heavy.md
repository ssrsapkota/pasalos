# Whitespace Heavy

**Keywords:** Gallery, Minimal, Luxury, Breathing room.

**Description:**
Extreme usage of padding and margins. Small text in a vast sea of white (or black). Implies confidence and luxury.

## Usage

### 1. Ratios
Content takes up 20%, space takes up 80%.

```html
<section class="h-screen w-full bg-white flex items-center justify-center">
    <div class="max-w-xs text-center">
        <h2 class="text-xs uppercase tracking-[0.3em] text-slate-400 mb-8">Collection 01</h2>
        <h1 class="text-2xl font-serif text-slate-900 leading-relaxed">
            True luxury is the absence of noise.
        </h1>
    </div>
</section>
```
