# Single Word

**Keywords:** Impact, Statement, Poster, Simplicity.

**Description:**
A single, massive word dominates the screen. It might be cropped, rotated, or filled with video. Subtext is minimal. "Less is more."

## Usage

### 1. Viewport Fit
Text that touches both sides.

```html
<div class="h-screen flex items-center justify-center overflow-hidden bg-black text-white">
    <h1 class="text-[35vw] font-black tracking-tighter leading-none select-none">
        NOW
    </h1>
    <div class="absolute bottom-12 text-center">
        <p class="uppercase tracking-widest text-xs">The future is irrelevant.</p>
    </div>
</div>
```
