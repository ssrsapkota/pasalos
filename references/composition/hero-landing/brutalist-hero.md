# Brutalist Hero

**Keywords:** Raw, Ugly-chic, System fonts, High contrast, Outlines.

**Description:**
Anti-design. Uses default system fonts (Times New Roman, Arial), distinct black borders, clashing colors (neons on grey), and raw HTML aesthetic.

## Usage

### 1. Border-Black & Monospace
The brutalist starter pack.

```html
<div class="bg-[#ccc] min-h-screen p-4 font-mono">
    
    <div class="border-4 border-black bg-white p-8 mb-4 shadow-[8px_8px_0px_0px_#000]">
        <h1 class="text-6xl font-bold uppercase border-b-4 border-black pb-4 mb-4">
            Brute.
        </h1>
        <p class="text-lg">No gradients. No shadows (soft ones). Just data.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-4">
        <div class="border-4 border-black bg-[#ff00ff] h-64 flex items-center justify-center text-4xl font-bold">
            A
        </div>
        <div class="border-4 border-black bg-[#00ff00] h-64 flex items-center justify-center text-4xl font-bold">
            B
        </div>
    </div>

</div>
```
