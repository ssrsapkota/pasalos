# Labels (UI Text)

**Keywords:** Microcopy, Forms, Badges, Pills, Metadata.

**Description:**
Label text is functional. It's not for "reading" in the traditional sense; it's for "identifying". It needs to be scanned instantly.

## Traits

### 1. Clarity
*   **Font:** Almost always Sans-serif (clearest shapes).
*   **Weight:** Often Medium or SemiBold (to stand out at small sizes).
*   **Case:** Often Uppercase (for structure) or Sentence Case (for friendliness).

### 2. Size
Small but legible. 11px - 14px range.

### 3. Usage Pattern
*   **Form Labels:** Above inputs. High contrast.
*   **Badges:** White text on colored bg. Bold.
*   **Metadata:** (e.g., "Posted 2h ago"). Low contrast gray.

## Tiff's Rule
"If it's small, make it bold (or uppercase). If it's big, make it thin."
