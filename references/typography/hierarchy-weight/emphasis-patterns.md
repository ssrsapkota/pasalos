# Emphasis Patterns

**Keywords:** Bold, Italic, Color, Highlight, Focus.

**Description:**
Emphasis is used to draw attention to specific words or phrases within a block of text. Standard patterns exist because users recognize them instantly.

## The Big Three

### 1. Bold (Strong)
*   **Meaning:** High importance, "Look at this".
*   **Usage:** Key terms, user names in a feed, defining a concept.
*   **Tag:** `<strong>` or `<b>`.

### 2. Italic (Emphasis)
*   **Meaning:** different tone, foreign word, title of work.
*   **Usage:** Book titles, internal monologue, subtle stress ("I said *no*").
*   **Tag:** `<em>` or `<i>`.
*   **Warning:** Harder to read on screens. Use sparingly.

### 3. Color / Highlight
*   **Meaning:** Actionable or Status.
*   **Usage:** Links (Blue), Errors (Red), Success (Green), Search matches (Yellow bg).

## Anti-Patterns
*   **Bold + Italic + Underline:** Too much. Pick one.
*   **Bold Paragraphs:** If everything is bold, nothing is bold.
*   **Underlining non-links:** Confuses users who think they can click it.
